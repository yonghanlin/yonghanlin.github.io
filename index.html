I am a senior student at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (former: National Chiao Tung University), majoring in computer science and working with Prof. Yung-Ju Chang at the Mobile Ubiquitous Interaction Lab (MUI). I create better systems that support human’s interaction with technology, and I want to design technology that mediates the interaction between individuals. My research interests lie in the fields of human-computer interaction (HCI) and social computing. Recently, I am doing research to improve my past research tool - NotiManager and deeply investigate how people manage their notifications by using NotiManager.

In the past, I was a research intern in Human-Centered Design Lab at Polytechnique Montréal, working with Prof. Jinghui Cheng. Also, I was a software engineering intern at Xaduro Inc., a tech startup.

Outside of research, I love to take photos of places I visit and read books.
