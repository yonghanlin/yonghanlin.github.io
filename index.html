I create better systems that support human’s interaction with technology, and I want to design technology that mediates the interaction between individuals.

My research interests lie in the fields of human-computer interaction (HCI) and social computing. Recently, I am doing research that investigates the interaction between individuals with notifications on their smartphones. Outside of work, I love to travel and take photos of places I visit.
