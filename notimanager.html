Pinning

To enable users to anchor specific notifications, we introduce the pinning feature. By pinning notifications, users can ensure that the pinned notification is kept and remains visible, unaffected by any actions such as clicking, swiping off, or pressing delete-all-notifications button. The pinned notification can only be removed after deselecting the pin. This feature is designed to allow users to retain notifications that they may wish to revisit or reference later within NotiManager
